THE CALM OF NIGHT!

As the sun dips below the Mumbai skyline, casting a warm orange glow across the city, the night unfurls its velvet cloak, transforming the bustling streets into a canvas of twinkling lights and hushed sounds. The air, once thick with the sounds of traffic and chatter, now carries the gentle hum of distant conversations and the rhythmic pulse of the city's nightlife. Street vendors set up their stalls, offering a tantalizing array of street food, while families stroll through the parks, enjoying the cool evening air. The stars begin to emerge, their brilliance enhanced by the darkness, and the city, once a vibrant tapestry of activity, settles into a peaceful slumber, awaiting the dawn's return.
